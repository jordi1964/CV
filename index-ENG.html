Responsibility

My priority is to always meet the objectives with the utmost rigor, excellence and quality at work.

Organization

I always try to have a good planning of the tasks to be carried out and be clear about the deadlines for each of them. From here on, I prioritize those that are most urgent at all times. I can perform several tasks simultaneously without problem.

Web design, front-end

With years of experience in the different fields of graphic design, as of 2017 I began to be interested in the creation of web pages, both from the point of view of design and implementation. I start looking for information on the internet and follow online courses in HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript.

As a result of the knowledge acquired, I take on two projects of different magnitude: the creation of a page for a newly created carpentry company, and the redesign of the website of a yoga center that already had one, but had become totally obsolete.

Disseny Maldà Recicla

Website created for my brother's company, dedicated to making furniture with recycled wood.

The objective of the page was to publicize the company and the catalog of products it offers.

La Tribuna yoga center

Unfinished redesign of the website of the yoga centers La Tribuna, in Olot, and El Pou de la Llum, in Figueres. The page they had had been totally obsolete and was not responsive.

My idea was to give it a more current aesthetic and at the same time improve functionality and usability, keeping all the sections of the previous version and adding new ones.

Willingness to learn

The world of graphic design is constantly evolving, not so much in its theoretical aspect as in the tools we use. Design and desktop publishing software continually provides improvements in which professionals have to adapt, either by searching for information online or through face-to-face courses.
